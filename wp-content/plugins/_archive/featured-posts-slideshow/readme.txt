Featured Posts Slideshow is a wonderfull Wordpress Javascript Slideshow. It displays as many Post's images as you want.

== Description ==

Featured Posts Slideshow shows as many post's images as you want. It's a very nice and powerfull Javascript Slideshow. It displays a certain image for every Post of a category that you have defined. You can slide through your Slideshow with the help of nice navigation buttons. *The images are directly linked to the specific Wordpress post*. 
You have a powerfull Administration Area to adjust the Category, the number of Posts, the width and height, and many colours for the Slideshow!

* Images from as many Posts as you want
* Navigation through left and right buttons
* Image for every different Post
* Powerfull Administration for Posts and Styles of the Slideshow
* Javascript Effects between sliding through Posts
* Images are linked directly to the specific Post

Check out the <a href="http://www.iwebix.de/featured-posts-slideshow-demo/" target="_blank">DEMO</a> at <a href="http://www.iwebix.de/" target="_blank">Webdesign</a>

== Installation ==

1. Upload /featured-posts-slideshow/ to your Plugin directory (wp-content/plugins/)
2. Go to the 'Plugins' Page of your Administration Area and activate the Plugin.
3. Go to Settings/Featured Posts Slideshow and customize the Slideshow for your needs.
3. Place `<?php include (ABSPATH . '/wp-content/plugins/featured-posts-slideshow/featured-posts.php'); ?>` in your template.
4. Add Custom Field to your Post with Key "thumbnail" and Value should be direct link to the image like "http://www.google.de/logo.png" (without quotation marks).

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where can I insert the Slideshow? =

YOu can Insert the Slideshow almost everywhere you want (it looks best under Navigation Bar or in Footer)!

= Where can I edit the Stylesheet? =

You don't have to edit the Stylesheet you can make changes directly in your Administration Panel (Settings --> Featured Posts Slideshow)

= What is perfect Resolution for the Images? =

Perfect resolution for Images is 60x60 Pixel but you can define a variable width.
